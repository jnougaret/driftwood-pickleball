Privacy Policy

Last updated: February 4, 2026

Driftwood Pickleball ("we", "our", "us") provides tournament registration and event management services. This page explains what data we collect, how we use it, and your choices.

Information We Collect

  • Account data from your sign-in provider (such as email and user ID).
  • Profile data you provide (display name and linked DUPR information, if connected).
  • Tournament data (registrations, teams, scores, and event participation history).
  • Basic technical logs needed for security and service operation.

How We Use Information

  • Operate and improve tournament registration and scoring features.
  • Display tournament participation and results to users.
  • Prevent misuse and maintain platform security.
  • Respond to support and account requests.

Sharing

We do not sell personal information. We share data only with service providers needed to run the platform (for example authentication, hosting, and database infrastructure).

Data Retention

We retain account and tournament data as needed to operate the service and maintain event history, unless a deletion request is submitted.

Your Rights

You may request access, correction, or deletion of your personal data by contacting us at info@driftwoodpickleball.com.

Contact

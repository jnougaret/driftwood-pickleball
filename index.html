About Driftwood Pickleball

Welcome to Driftwood Pickleball, Maine's community-driven pickleball tournament organizer. Like driftwood shaped by the sea—weathered, tough, and full of character—our players have washed up to find their best game in pickleball.

The Harbor Tournament Series features low-cost, player-focused tournaments where 100% of entry fees are returned as prize money. No taxation, all celebration.

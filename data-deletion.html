Data Deletion Instructions

Last updated: February 4, 2026

To request deletion of your Driftwood Pickleball account data, email info@driftwoodpickleball.com with the subject line: Data Deletion Request.

Please include the email address used to sign in.

What We Delete

  • Account profile information tied to your user record.
  • Tournament registration entries associated with your account, where possible.
  • Linked authentication identifiers stored in our database.

What May Be Retained

  • Minimal records required for legal, security, or fraud-prevention obligations.
  • Historical tournament result snapshots that are no longer linked to identifying account data.

Processing Time

Requests are typically completed within 30 days.
